Hi, I’m Jason Chen, a Computer Engineering student at Simon Fraser University.

I am interested in Embodied AI, LLMs, AI agent systems, computing acceleration, and hardware-aware AI systems. I like building systems that connect perception, reasoning, planning, and real-world hardware constraints.

My recent work includes robot learning experiments with a dexterous robotic hand, edge AI model deployment on Raspberry Pi, hardware acceleration projects, and an autonomous robot and AI agent system with LLM-based command understanding and human confirmation. I am also supporting a professor research project related to AI on hardware and model training.

This site is where I document my projects, technical notes, and lessons learned from building real engineering systems.
